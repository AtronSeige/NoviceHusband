kiwi

“Kia Ora Aotearoa!”

Kia Ora means Hello in Māori, and Aotearoa means Long White Cloud, which is the Māori name for New Zealand.

Tanja got a work offer that allowed us to move to New Zealand. So, in 2019 we packed a few things, sold the rest and moved to Christchurch at the end of 2019.

I was still employed at a South African company, but was lucky enough to get employment by late February 2020.

Both of us was still settling into our new positions when the COVID-19 lockdown was put into force. Luckily for us we are comfortable around each other and comfortable working from home.

And that is how we got to where we are now. We hope to make this beautiful country our home.
