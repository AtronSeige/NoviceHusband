All Go Cleaner

All Go was suggested to me by a local brewer in Chch. According to them and the manufacturer it is used by a lot of the commercial breweries in New Zealand.

The reason I decided to find something other than PBW was the cost. Imported cleaning materials are quite expensive in NZ, so using something local made sense.

I have found that I can re-use the solution many times. See disclaimer.

WARNING

THIS CAN KILL YOU! It is highly corrosive and toxic. This is a very basic material, so you need an acid to counter it. I always have a 2L bottle of vinegar nearby.

Use of PPE while using this is highly recommended.

Contact the manufacturer to get a safety data sheet.

First Aid (Personal Experience)

  1. Immediately remove all contaminated clothing
  2. Rinse skin with vinegar
  3. Rinse skin with water or shower

Important Numbers

  • National Poison Centre 0800764766
  • Ambulance/Police 111

Dilution

The suggested dilution is 5-10 ml per liter of water.

Quick Reference

All Go Water Usage
150ml 20L Keg
150ml 20L Grainfather G40
225ml 30L Fermentor

Preparation

  1. Determine the amount of detergent that will be required
  2. Measure out the water
  3. Measure out the All Go and add to the water
  4. Heat to 60C

Usage

  1. If possible, start heating the diluted All Go before the rest of the steps
  2. Dump out any residual content
  3. Rinse with water and try to remove as much residue as possible
  4. Any hard or stubborn residue should be removed with a soft cloth. Scrubs can be used, but not on plastic
  5. Ensure that all taps and valves are closed
  6. Add the hot diluted All Go
  7. Leave for 10 to 20 minutes. Pump or stir occasionally if possible
  8. (Personal Choice) If the diluted mix is still clear, transfer the content to storage containers (See disclaimer)
  9. Discard the diluted mix
  10. Rinse the vessel

Re-Use

I have found that the diluted mix can be stored and re-used. If the mix is still quite clear after use I will store it for re-use. The moment it is not clear I will discard it.
I discard the mix if it has been used six times or for three brew days.

Disclaimer

  • This is based on my experience of using the solution in my home brewery
  • Do not sue me or EcoChem if you follow my re-use instructions
